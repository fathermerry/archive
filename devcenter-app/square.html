The Open Source Movement?
Really, why do Open Source?

Contributing to Open source projects gives you the opportunity to do stuff with some really great people and make connections outside your normal circle of friends and co-workers. More importantly, you develop new skills and talent and gain invaluable experience. In addition to developing technical skills, you also experience how to function as part of a team. And, finally it is something that can help you land your next job or inspire your next big idea. You do not know where the Journey will take you.
