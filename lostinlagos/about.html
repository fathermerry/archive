Discover Everything

Living in Lagos may not be easy, but it doesn’t have to be hard

Living in Lagos may not be easy, but it doesn’t have to be hard. There is more to Lagos than meets the eye, when it comes to Lagos you just have to know where to look, and I’ve been looking hard. So for all of you who think there is nothing in Lagos I want to prove you wrong.

After digging deep I’ve found some hidden gems in La La Land, and I want to share them, and many more, with you. I’ve put together this website to help you all discover Lagos the way I have, with the most comprehensive list of Lagos’ restaurants, shops and more. So next time you are stuck for a restaurant, a place for a coffee, a drink at the end of a long and hectic Lagos week, look no further than Lost in Lagos. And of course, if you’re looking for a gift, a new dress, a new piece of furniture, then I will tell you where you can find it.

Every month I will feature a place as the Spot of the Month that I think you should all visit. Keep in an eye out for the best events in Lagos, especially the Lost in Lagos LIVE events.

And of course, I don’t know everywhere, so if I’ve missed a place please don’t hesitate to let me know and I’ll be sure to add it. I hope you discover the Lagos that I’ve had the pleasure of getting to know.

Tannaz Bahnam, Managing Director
