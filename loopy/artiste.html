Loose Kaynon , known to family as Joakim Ikhazoboh grew up in a household that celebrated good music as often. He remembers that his dad had the largest record collection he’s ever seen. His keen interest in music honed in on rap and hip-hop as he listened to Jay Z, Notorious BIG, Big Daddy Kane and Eminem.

He began pursuing music professionally in 2006 while studying for his degree in banking and finance. Over the last couple of years, Loose Kaynon had built a steady fan base by stellar performances on his singles and appearances on a number of other artistes’ projects.

Loose Kaynon founded the number one weekly hip-hop show in Lagos, Nigeria, called Wax Lyrical, which gives local talents a chance to express themselves and showcase skill.
