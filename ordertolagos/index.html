How it works

STEP 1

Create an Order

Create a shipping order for the products that you want to buy from overseas. A shipping order consists of a list of products, each one with a title, a link to the website where you want to buy it from and details of the order.
When you're done, send the order. You can create an order without an account, but an account lets you save orders as draft and edit later before sending.

STEP 2

Billing

After sending your order, we review the products you've added. Then we reply to the specified email address with an invoice showing it'll cost you to ship your goods in an email including a unique link to fulfil your order.

STEP 3

Delivery

Once billing is done and the order has been paid for, we start processing the delivery of your items, ensuring that we keep you up-to-date with the progress of your parcel. We deliver to your doorstep.
