About Us

haralago is a company that
provides non-clinical support services
to healthcare facilities

haralago's mission is to help our clients to deliver high quality care to their patients by relieving them of the burden of non-clinical operations

Our mission is to take care of the needs of the clinical staff, so that the clinical staff can take care of the patient.
